MMR Media Group:

The Late Shri. Veerappa P. Madlur Founder: Group of MMR MEDIA Publications MMR MEDIA is the first South India's Commodity Market Newspaper. It was launched in 1975. It was published in Mangalore in the Name of “MANGALORE MARKET REPORTS”, over the years it spread out across Karnataka and Printed in 4 cities like Mangalore, Bangalore, Shimoga and Hubli. The MMR MEDIA passed another milestone in 1993 by launching A Tamil Edition in Chennai, In the name of “MOOKAMBIKA MARKET REVIEWS”. It was the First Commodity newspaper in Tamilnadu. MMR media uses modern facilities for news gathering and it remains as a favorite newspaper to the readers throughout Tamil Nadu. MMR Media launched as a Telugu edition “MOOKAMBIKA MARKET REVIEWS” from Hyderabad in Telangana. This edition addresses the long-felt needs of readers in both Andhrapradesh and Telangana state, providing them authentic news and objective views. Now, with these editions of four states we proudly say it has become the most popular publication in South India.
