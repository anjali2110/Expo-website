Exhibition Venue

  • Being on the success of three exhibitions, we have decided to organize our 4th and 5th International “Mookambika Rice & Pulses Tech expo-2018” to be held on 02, 03 & 04th February 2018, at Shubham Convention Centre, Nagole, L.B. Nagar, Hyderabad, Telangana State, and on 05th, 6th & 7th April 2018 at Maniram Dewan Trade Centre , Guwahati, Assam State, India respectively. We hope that this Expo will be surely benefitable to everyone to show their products and Brands and it will make direct contact with new potential customers and creating business opportunities to increase your business ratio. We are expecting more than 2000 visitors will be participated in the expo.

Event Promotion
